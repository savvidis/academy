The Fullstack Flex Immersive

Fullstack's Flex Immersive allows you to study fullstack web development, while keeping your existing job and salary.

Fullstack's Flex Immersive is a program uniquely designed for working professionals. Flex covers the same curriculum as our popular full-time immersive program, but it is structured in a part-time weekend/nights format over 16 weeks.

You will attend class at the Fullstack campus on Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday evenings and all day on Saturday. On Monday evening and Sunday, you will work from home, watching lectures and code projects and tutorials in preparation for the upcoming class session. Whether you are at home or in class, Fullstack Instructors will be available to help you!

The Program

Our goal is to make you a strong professional developer—good enough that we'd want to hire you at our own startups.

# Structure

The Fullstack curriculum is divided into three major courses: Foundations, the Academy and Flight.

Fullstack Foundations

The first part of your educational journey at Fullstack, Foundations, is an intensive introductory course designed to help you master the core concepts of programming. Foundations is not just a list of links to online tutorials. You'll be working on a targeted core curriculum with live instructors and real coding projects. You'll be prepared to hit the ground running when you come to campus.

Fullstack Academy - Flex Immersive

Fullstack Flex Immersive covers the same curriculum as our full-time course, but it is structured in a part-time format over 16 weeks. You'll build many fullstack applications: using MongoDB for data storage, Express and Node.js on the backend, and AngularJS on the front-end to create dynamic and real-time application interfaces.

Fullstack Flight

In the 2nd half of your course, our placement team will begin matching your interests with employers — a process that will continue after graduation. With our dedicated training program on technical interviewing and becoming a star developer, we'll make sure you're ready for whatever your next step after Fullstack is.

# What will I learn?

Fundamentals

We build a solid core of programming fundamentals to catalyze a long and productive career in software development. Master the technologies that are currently in-demand and gain the foundation to learn the innovative technologies of the future.

FullStack Web Development

Our namesake is our promise. You'll learn end-to-end web development using Node.js/Express, JavaScript, Angular.js, HTML5, and CSS3. You'll develop and hone your code-sense for seeing problems and imagining innovative ways to solve them in code.

Best Practices and Tools

Professional development is a lot more than just writing code. You'll learn best-practices and how to use all the right tools, from source control to web inspector to analytics.

Developer Culture

Fullstack will immerse you in one of the most vibrant tech communities in the world. You'll contribute to open source, attend tech meetups, win hackathons and build a strong network in Silicon Alley.

FUllSTACK FLEX FAQ

Q1. What are the dates of the Flex Program?

Our next class of Fullstack Flex will begin January 2015.

Q2. Can I keep working during the Flex Immersive?

Yes, the Flex Immersive is designed to allow you to keep working during the course. That said, the course will be just as intensive as the full-time program (if not more) and so be prepared to dedicate most of your evenings and weekends to coding.

Q3. Do I have to participate in Hiring Day after Fullstack?

You will have the opportunity to participate in a Hiring Day after Fullstack if you wish to explore development positions, however, there is no requirement to do so. You are welcome to apply your code-ninja skills to make your existing job more awesome!

Q4. How long is the Fullstack Flex Class?

The Fullstack Flex Immersive course is 16 weeks long.

Q5. What does the program cost?

Tuition is $12,500 for the semester. Fullstack also offers the Ada Lovelace Scholarship for Women which is a $1,000 scholarship to encourage women to attend.

Q6. I have other questions, whom do I contact?

We are happy to answer any further questions you have, email us at flex@fullstackacademy.com.
