The CTO Program

Ready. Set. Launch.

At Fullstack Academy, we’re focused on more than just getting you your first job in the tech world. We’ve started and sold companies, built technology teams and scaled up tech businesses. We know your dreams don’t stop at making your first app. Our CTO Program goes beyond just software and gets you ready to become a leader in the exciting and in-demand software development industry.

THE CTO PROGRAM

Learning to Learn

The one constant in technology is change. Understand more than just what technologies are now, understand where they are going and how to react, learn and utilize them effectively in your career. Our curriculum is also developed to accommodate the various types of learners out there. We combine various techniques so regardless of your learning style, we have an approach and curriculum roadmap for you.

Learning to Lead

Our graduates can jump into technology teams and start contributing right away. You'll understand what technology executives are looking for in their top performers and how to become a "star contributor." We'll use various project management and collaboration tools throughout the semester so you become familiar with the working styles of the modern tech team.

Learning by Teaching

We strongly believe in the pedagogical philosophy of Jean-Pol Martin "Learning by Teaching." Teaching others a skill that you have learned cements the idea into your mind through preparation of the idea, expression into language and discovery through questioning. Throughout the Fullstack Academy semester, we'll be using aspects of our own software to accelerate this process as well as give you opportunities to teach others in the community.

Become The Right Person

We believe everyone can be a software developer. The belief that you've started too late, a career switch is too difficult or that your brain just isn't wired for coding stems from fear and not reality. If you have an analytical mind, the passion for learning and an ethic of hard work, we can help you develop the right mindset to enhance, improve and accelerate your learning.

Real World Experience

"Can't get a job without experience, can't get experience without a job." We've seen people who have become amazing developers struggle at the beginning of their careers this classic chicken-and-egg problem. Through Fullstack Foundry, we will help you get connected with organizations that will give you the experience and credentials you need to land that first dream job.

Code Is A Communitiy

"Free as in beer", "Open Source", "Cathedral versus the Bazaar" - contributing to the community of coders is as old as the profession itself. Part of the curriculum of Fullstack Academy is being part of this community through contributing to open source projects and giving presentations to the NYC tech community. It not only is fun and the right thing to do, it's also great to help you land that first job.

Innovation Happens Everywhere

The Web is one of the most amazing technologies ever built by mankind but it's not the only game in town. We'll use our coding skills to solve all kinds of problems using devices like the Raspberry Pi, Arduino and Mobile.

StackUp Support

We want you to have a long career after your time in the Fullstack Academy. Fullstack has classes, networking events and continuing education available for all alumni of our program. We'll keep you up-to-date so you can continue to "StackUp" against your competition.
