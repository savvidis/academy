The Fullstack Foundry

Cutting-edge Knowledge. Real World Application.

The Fullstack Foundry connects current Fullstack students with real-world projects. We graduate students who can be productive on day one of their first job out of Fullstack. However, we found that many are ready to work on real-world projects during their time here — so why not get them hacking? We see it as a win-win; you get your project built and our students get to work on production code with companies that have real problems.

# What We Bring to the Table

Fullstack JavaScript

Our students are trained in fullstack web development in JavaScript. Want them to do front-end work in Angular? Sure. Need someone on the backend who's familiar with Node.js and MongoDB? We got you covered there too.

Low Risk, High Returns

We know it can be hard to find great demo projects for your platform, and hiring a full time developer to work on this new feature might be out of your reach for right now. Our program allows you to get the work you need done in a fixed amount of time by Fullstack students who are being mentored by trained instructors. Not happy with the results? We'll offer you a full refund.

Great People

Our admission process is highly selective and screens candidates for intelligence, passion, positive attitude and work ethic. We admit people who are at the top of their game and are ready to make this next step. We promise you'll enjoy working with our students.

# What You Bring to the Table

A Great Project

Have an idea that you are dying to have built, but don't have the bandwidth? Maybe you aren't sure what your idea is, but you are interested in working with our students. In either case, we can probably help you out.

A Little Bit of Time

We ask that you commit to three meetings with our students; one at the beginning of the project, one after the first week and one at the conclusion/hand-off. These times will give you an opportunity to explain your ideas, needs, and resources. Before the end of the project phase we ask that you meet for a final review to give our students the opportunity to make any necessary changes.

# Previous Projects

DonorsChoose.org

DonorsChoose.org worked with two Fullstack students on a project called "Needometer" that allowed visitors to their site to see D3 visualizations of the different kinds of needs across states. Our students worked with their Data Science team to access 12 years of data recently released by donorschoose.org.

Coder's Bracket

Love college basketball + programming? Coder's Bracket is your dream come true. Coder's Bracket allows users to write a JavaScript function that generates a March Madness bracket. Built using full-stack JavaScript, Coder's Bracket spent 18 hours on the Hacker News front page and had over 50,000 brackets generated.

Hire.FullstackAcademy.com

Sick of switching between LinkedIn, GitHub, and paper resumes? Fret no more, our students built hire.fullstackacademy which brings it all together in one easy place. A clean interface combined with easy to access information gives you the information you need. Built using Angular.js, Mongo & Node.js/Express.
